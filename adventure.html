ここは、ブーハーの世界の中で、私が生徒に直接話すページです。

This is The Booha Adventure, and it connects to the world of my novel Karasuki. Once you enter, you can review, listen, and practice all the weekly content without going any further. There are three curricula to choose from.

This app isn't connected to AI or any external API. Everything is local. Everything you read, see, and hear lives inside the app. From the study deck page, you can even chat with Uhibon. He knows everything about the app—and nothing about anything else. LOL.

I designed this app to be explored and to get lost in. The further you go, the more you find. Click the maze and you'll see the Booha Ghost flying around. One of them will meet you at the start and guide you to this week's games. Each curriculum has its own set of 9 games and its own theme. The more you play, the more unlocks in Karasuki. That's all I'm going to say.

Play a game and travel to the Karasuki tree. Remember—most trees are doorways to something.

You'll find your Adventure Profile page in Karasuki. Only you can see it. No one else—not even me—can see your progress or scores. You're in charge of your motivation and your output.

I built this app for my output-based school. Output is the language you produce—not what you read aloud or memorize, but what you actually use to communicate. Most students use Boo-riculum. All curricula overlap, so they can be combined. They loop and build practical conversational English without locking you into a fixed level. Your level is based on how much you use the language.

Each curriculum shows three weeks of study decks at a time: this week, last week, and next week. Each week includes 15 vocabulary words, 15 sentences, and 15 questions, all with full audio and Japanese translations, plus a hands-free stream for listening and repeating.

Adults
  • Come prepared. The goal is to move away from scripts and into real communication.
  • Common habits that slow progress: speaking English with Japanese logic, wanting perfect lines instead of real conversations, filling every answer with vague words like enjoy, famous, so-so, or busy, and having little curiosity about English-speaking culture.
  • Adults improve fastest when they talk about what they actually think, not just where they went and what they ate.
Study Decks The study decks are the foundation of everything. Nothing else works properly without them. Every week includes vocabulary cards, sentence cards, and question cards, all with full audio, along with a hands-free stream for listening and repeating.

The vocabulary cards train your ear and eyes to recognize the week's core words quickly. The sentence cards show how those words live inside real English. The question cards prepare you to respond, not just understand, so when it's time to speak you're not starting from zero.

The hands-free stream is where everything connects. You listen, repeat, and move with the language without stopping. This is where rhythm, confidence, and flow build.

These decks are not something you finish once. Come back to them until the English feels familiar.

Game Decks Each week includes 9 games built around the same English you studied.

The games are not separate from the study. They are where you use it. When the study decks feel familiar, the games feel smooth. When you skip the decks, the games feel hard. The system is honest like that.

As you complete games, more things begin to appear and unlock across the Adventure. The more you play, the more the world responds.

  • 準備して来てください。台本を読むのではなく、本当に話す練習をしましょう。

スタディデッキ スタディデッキはブーハーの土台です。ここをしっかりやっておかないと、他がうまくいきません。毎週、単語カード・文カード・質問カードが入っていて、すべて音声付きです。ハンズフリーで聞いてリピートできるストリームもあります。

単語カードは、その週のキーワードを音と文字の両方で素早く覚えるためのもの。文カードは、その単語が実際の英語の中でどう使われるかを見せてくれます。質問カードは、「分かる」じゃなくて「答える」ための準備です。ゲームで急に詰まらないように、先に英語を体に入れておきます。

ストリームはその全部をつなぐ場所です。止めずに聞いて、繰り返して、英語のリズムに乗っていく。ここで自信とスピードがついてきます。

1回やったら終わり、じゃありません。「見たことある」「聞いたことある」になるまで、何度でも開いてください。

ゲームデッキ 毎週、同じ英語を使った9つのゲームが用意されています。

ゲームは勉強とは別のものじゃありません。ゲームが、使う場所です。スタディデッキに慣れていると、ゲームはスムーズに進みます。飛ばすと、急に難しく感じます。ブーハーはそういうところが正直です。

ゲームを進めるほど、アドベンチャーの中でいろんなものが現れたり、解放されたりしていきます。やればやるほど、世界が動きます。
